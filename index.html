My Dearest Mahal

Two years ago sinagot mo ako sa taas ng Ferris Wheel, naalala ko pa yung time na kasama kita sa taas kasabay ng sunset, sinagot mo ako sa panliligaw ko sa'yo. Alam ko napaka rami kong pag kakamali na nagawa, yung mga panahon na nasaktan kita at nagalit ka saken at mga maliliit na bagay na di ko magawa para sa'yo. Sa lahat ng yon pinili mo na mag stay, at bigyan ako ng pagkakataon, para mapakita ko na bumabawi sa'yo. I am so deeply grateful for your love, your patience, and your strength na kayanin lahat ng iyon. You make me want to be a better man, the man you deserve, and I promise to keep working to show just how much I cherish you. These 2 years na officially na naging tayo at mga buwan, linggo at araw na pinag samahan natin bago moko sagutin, pinakita mo kung gaano moko kamahal. You shown me that love is not just about the perfect momments. It's about standing together when things are hard, forgiving wheb it's painful, and choosing each other over and over again. And every day I choose you. Thank you for being my greatest blessing, mahal, my love, my enemy, my forever partner. I look forward forward to every beautiful momments we'll create together. Let's make this next year even more amazing than the last. Filled with more joy, more adventure, and a love that grows even deeper. Happy Anniversary, mahal ko. I love you more than words can ever say. I LOVE YOU! LABLAB VinVin
